About Visiter

India has a myriad of landscapes, great heritage and culture, varied flora and fauna. The country is the most preferred tourist destinations for tourists from all across the world for its picturesque landscapes, spectacular waterfalls, habitat of the country's largest tiger reserve and home to the warmest people on earth.

Visiter is travel platform website designed to help travellers from all over the world. It provide free information about travelling and related bussiness. We believe in, "Simple living and High exploring". As the whole world knows about India's diverse culture and delicacies but it's real beauty, it's heritage is still in the depth of darkness. Through this site we want to emit light into the darkness and let the world behold its genuine beauty, its splendid architecture,its eye soothing beaches and to show the pursuit of brave maharajas through their indestructible forts and palaces.

"Travel is not reward for working, it's education for living "
